Peanut Chicken Curry

Description

This is a classic West African sauce that goes well with rice. All credit goes to Oumou from recettesafricaine.com

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Peanut Butter (preferably smooth and unsalted)
  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 1 Tablespoon tomato concentrate
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Sliced green oniions
  • A handful of basil leaves and parsley
  • *optional* 1 large pepper
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. In a large pot, fry the chicken until golden with about 100 ml of cooking oil(vegetable/canola). Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. In a food processor blitz garlic, peppers, and herbs and mix with onions and green onions. Don't blend too much just enough for everthing to come together
  3. Mix tomato concentrate into a cup of water and add vegetable blend
  4. Heat rest of cooking oil and pour in tomato mix let it fry off for 10 minutes. Meanwhile mix peanut butter into 1l of water until homogenized pour it into tomato sauce after the 10 min
  5. Add another liter of water and season to taste with salt and pepper. You can also add a bouillon cube for extra flavor
  6. Let simmer uncovered for about 30 minutes on medium heat, stirring occasionally
  7. *Optional* After 30 minutes you can add in some extra vegetables if you'd like (carrots,bell peppers, potato..etc) and let cook 15 more minutes
  8. When its almost done lower heat and let simmer until oil rises to the top
  9. Serve with rice and enjoy!!
